# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Atlaix Max serves crypto traders, wallet researchers, and token-risk analysts who need to inspect wallet behavior, token holdings, alerts, scans, and market signals while deciding what deserves attention.

## Product Purpose

Atlaix Max brings wallet intelligence, token discovery, safe-scan data, smart-money signals, watchlists, alerts, and assistant workflows into one operational dashboard. Success means users can judge wallet and token behavior from live data without switching tools or trusting vague scores.

## Brand Personality

Focused, direct, technical. The product should feel like a calm trading desk: dense enough for serious work, clear enough to trust quickly, and restrained in its visual choices.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page composition inside the app, decorative crypto hype, over-rounded glass panels, vague AI copy, fake precision, and bright casino-like trading screens. Do not turn wallet intelligence into raw transaction dumps without summaries, context, and confidence.

## Design Principles

- Put live evidence before claims.
- Keep dense data scannable through hierarchy, alignment, and stable table behavior.
- Preserve user trust by showing unavailable, inferred, partial, and provider-reported data clearly.
- Favor fast operational workflows over decorative presentation.
- Keep the interface consistent across dashboard, wallet, scan, alert, and assistant surfaces.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA for contrast, focus states, keyboard access, labels, and reduced-motion tolerance. Color must not be the only indicator for gain, loss, status, risk, or confidence.
